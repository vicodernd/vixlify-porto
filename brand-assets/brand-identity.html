The logo

The Lift Mark is three ascending chevrons — a visual echo of amplify / lift, the "‑lify" in Vixlify. It is drawn, not typed: a custom geometric mark that reads as motion and upward momentum. Pair it with the wordmark for the primary lockup, or use the mark alone where space is tight.

Construction & clear space

Built on a 64×64 grid, symmetrical around the vertical axis. The mark widens as it descends, so the eye is pulled upward.

  • Stroke: rounded caps and joins, weight ≈ 10% of the mark height.
  • Clear space: keep margins equal to the height of one chevron on all sides.
  • Minimum size: 20 px (favicon) for the mark, 96 px wide for the full lockup.
02

Keep it clean

The mark earns its premium feel by staying disciplined. A few things to never do.

✓ Do

Use the gradient on solid dark, or the mono / reversed versions on busy or colored backgrounds.

✕ Don't

Re-introduce the old rounded-square badge or place the mark inside a container box.

✓ Do

Preserve the chevron proportions and rounded stroke exactly as drawn.

✕ Don't

Stretch, rotate, recolor with off-brand hues, add drop shadows, or sharpen the corners.

05

Voice & motion

Confident, precise, never loud. We sound like a craftsperson who has already done the thinking.

We are precise

Specific claims, concrete nouns, short sentences. We name the outcome, not the effort.

Not hype-driven

No buzzwords, no exclamation marks, no "revolutionary." The work carries the weight.

Motion has a job

Every animation guides the eye, builds trust, or closes the gap to a yes. Cubic-bezier(0.4, 0, 0.2, 1), 0.4–0.6s.

Not decoration

No motion for its own sake. If a transition doesn't aid comprehension, it gets cut.
